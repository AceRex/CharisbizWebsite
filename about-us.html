About Us

Charisbiz Africa started on October 2018, out of a great passion to see startup businesses succeed. Having studied the trend and realised that Startups collapse within the first 5 years of their existence due to numerous factors, we decided to give Startups all the support they need in form of strategies and idea creation. We believe great thinking will translate to great products. We operate in line with goals 4, 8 and 17 of the SDGs, and our services are geared towards providing clarity, innovation, structure and sustainable development for startup businesses.

MOTIVATION

With the current median age of Nigeria at 18 years, and that of Africa at 19.4 years, there is a desperate need for resourceful individuals who can help provide lasting solution to the ever arising crisis facing the country continent. Knowing that Africa is a growing economy and as such, businesses must start, grow and thrive, it is important to invest heavily in and aggressively develop the human capital of this youthful continent, starting with our immediate space. We at Charisbiz Africa want to be part of their success story.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

At Charisbiz Africa, we believe if we will have a progressive continent with creativity, innovation and a booming economy which can rival the rest of the world, there must first be an upgrade in the mind and an expansion of knowledge base.
